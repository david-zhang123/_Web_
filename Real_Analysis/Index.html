Introduction To Proofs

Mathematics is a fundamental science that seeks to describe and explain the natural world using logic and quantitative reasoning. A key aspect of mathematics is the use of proofs, which are formal demonstrations that establish the truth or validity of mathematical statements. From an analytic standpoint, proofs are central to the discipline of mathematics as they provide a rigorous and systematic way to establish the correctness of mathematical ideas.
